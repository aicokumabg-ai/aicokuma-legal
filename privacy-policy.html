Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how aicokuma ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our TikTok comment automation service. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account to the App, we may access the following data through TikTok's official API:

  • Your TikTok user ID and username (for authentication purposes)
  • Comments on your TikTok videos (to detect specified keywords)
  • TikTok user IDs of commenters (to send automated direct messages)

We do not collect passwords, payment information, or sensitive personal data.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account via the official API.
  • Monitor comments on your videos for keywords you specify.
  • Send automated direct messages to users who trigger those keywords.
  • Improve the functionality and reliability of the App.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store only the minimum data necessary to operate the service. Access tokens provided by TikTok are stored securely and used only to perform authorized actions on your behalf. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • As required by law or legal process.
  • To TikTok, as necessary to use their API (governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy).
  • To service providers who assist in operating the App, under strict confidentiality agreements.

6. TikTok Data

Data obtained through TikTok's API is used in accordance with TikTok's API Terms of Service and Platform Policy. We do not use TikTok user data for advertising or any purpose beyond operating the App's stated functions.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service. When you disconnect your TikTok account or delete your account, we delete your associated data within 30 days.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent and disconnect your TikTok account at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@aicokuma.com
